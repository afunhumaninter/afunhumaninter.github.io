What is DiscriminatoryValues?

DiscriminatoryValues is a political quiz that attempts to assign percentages to different values based on discrimination. You will be presented by a statement, and then you will answer with your opinion on the statement, from Strongly Agree to Strongly Disagree, with each answer slightly affecting your scores. At the end of the quiz, your answers will be compared to the maximum possible for each value, thus giving you a percentage. Answer honestly!

There are questions in the test.

What are the twelve values?


WHITE SUPREMACY

White nationalists/supremacists believe in the notion that white people are inherently superior to other races (black for the sake of this test).

RACISM

BLACK SUPREMACY

Black nationalists/supremacists believe in the notion that black people are inherently superior to other races (white for the sake of this test).

MATRIARCHY

Matriarchists believe that social customs and/or laws should benefit the woman more than the man.

SEXISM

PATRIARCHY

Patriarchists believe that social customs and/or laws should benefit the man more than the woman.

JEUNISM

Jeunists believe that the law of the land should give younger people more rights than adults or older people.

AGEISM

ADULTISM

Adultists believe that the law of the land should give adults or older people more rights than younger people.

HETERONORMATISM

Heteronormative people believe that social customs, traditions, and even laws should favor heterosexual lifestyles/individuals over homosexual lifestyles/individuals.

SEXUAL ORIENTATION

HOMONORMATISM

Homonormative people believe that social customs, traditions, and even laws should favor homosexual lifestyles/individuals over heterosexual lifestyles/individuals.

CISNORMATISM

Cisnormative people believe that gender identity should be fully based on chromosomes and birth gender. Usually, people in this category believe trans people are invalid.

GENDER IDENTITY

TRANSNORMATISM

Transnormative people believe that cisgendered people/cisgenderism should be looked down upon or even discriminated against. People in this category believe trans people are superior.

THIN-SUPREMACY

Fatphobic people believe people who are not able-bodied and/or obese are inferior to thin people. People here believe there is nothing wrong with fatshaming.

BODY TYPE

FAT-SUPREMACY

Thinphobic people believe fat people are naturally superior to thin people and that thinshaming should replace fatshaming.
